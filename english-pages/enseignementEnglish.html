Activités d’enseignement :

Module: Photovoltaic Systems (5th year of the GE Electrical Engineering sector): (Course, DW, PW)

1stElement of the module: Basic semiconductor materials:
  • Introduction to the physics of crystalline semiconductors: band structure, doping.
  • Phénomènes de transport, jonction p-n et effet photovoltaïque
  • Transport phenomena, p-n junction and photovoltaic effect
  • Electrical properties of semiconductors out of thermodynamic equilibrium.
  • PN junctions at equilibrium and out of thermodynamic equilibrium
  • Radiation-Matter Interaction
  • Basic semiconductor materials for photovoltaics
  • Les photopiles à base de semi-conducteurs cristallins
  • Solar cells based on crystalline semiconductors
  • Thin-film solar cells and heterojunctions
2nd Module element: Photovoltaic cells and systems
  • Principle of operation of the photovoltaic cell
  • Photovoltaic yield
  • The different types of photovoltaic cells
  • Theoretical description and modeling of the PV cell (Ideal PV cell; real PV cell)
  • Effect of climatic variations on the photovoltaic cell: variation in illumination; temperature variation.
  • Effect of inclination and orientation on the performance of PV panels.
  • The GPV photovoltaic generator: introduction; Grouping of photovoltaic cells in series, in parallel, mixed grouping; characterization and modeling of a GPV (electrical characteristics; efficiency of a GPV);
  • The principles of conversion in PV systems (direct coupling of a PV generator and a resistive load; adaptation stage between a PV generator and a load; DC/DC converter).
  • Direct coupling between a GPV and a battery.
  • The MPPT converter: introduction; MPP research principle; study of the operation of the static converter (buck converter, efficiency of the static converter);
  • Maximum power point tracking MPPT commands; quality criterion of an MPPT command; efficiency of the power chain; MPPT algorithms (Perturbation and Observation Algorithm (P&O); conductance incrementation algorithm (IncCond)
  • Sizing of an autonomous PV installation or connected to the electrical network

Module: Electrokinetics (1st year of the STPI sector (CP1)): (Practical work)

PW1 : STUDY OF THE POTENTIOMETRIC ASSEMBLY
  • Know how to use the voltage divider;
  • Know the limits of use
PW2 : RESISTANCE MEASUREMENT
  • Determine the values ​​of unknown resistances by direct application of Ohm's law using the short and long shunt assemblies;
  • Study the assemblies mentioned above;
  • Check the resistance association laws
PW3 : WHEATSTONE BRIDGE

The purpose of this manipulation is to determine the values ​​of two unknown resistors by the WHEATSTONE bridge method

PW4 : STUDY AND USE OF AN OSCILLOSCOPE
  • Visualize variations in electrical potential as a function of time.
  • Measure the potential difference (d.d.p.) of an electrical signal, its period T ….
  • Compare two signals (amplitudes and phase shift…).
PW5 : TRANSIENT CIRCUIT ANALYSIS

Study the behavior of 1st order and 2nd order circuits in transient state.
